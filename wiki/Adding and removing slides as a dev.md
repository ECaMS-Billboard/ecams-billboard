Adding and removing slides as a developer: 

ADDING
step 1: get your email added to the list of approved emails by someone already on the project. (should be completed already for all members of Stone Ocean)
step 2: navigate to https://ecams-bb-main-api-b5eebnawg4efapek.centralus-01.azurewebsites.net/ on a browser, you may be prompted to login, login using your email that was used in step 1 ( Lewis emails for stone Ocean members)
step 3: click on the slides button.
step 4: click on the "choose a file" button and select a png file of the slide you wish to add.
step 5: click on the "add slide" button
step 6: assuming steps 1-5 have been done correctly, if you scroll down to the bottom of the slides page, you should see your new slide pending approval.
step 7: click approve in order to add it. 
step 8: Wait ~ 15 minutes in order to let the billboard pull the new set of slides and start displaying them.

REMOVING 
step 1: get your email added to the list of approved emails by someone already on the project. (should be completed already for all members of Stone Ocean)
step 2: navigate to https://ecams-bb-main-api-b5eebnawg4efapek.centralus-01.azurewebsites.net/ on a browser, you may be prompted to login, login using your email that was used in step 1 ( Lewis emails for stone Ocean members)
step 3: click on the slides button.
step 4: scroll down until you find the slide you wish to remove
step 5: press the "decline" button associated with that slide. 
step 6: Wait ~ 15 minutes in order to let the billboard pull the new set of slides and start displaying them.
